The Backstage of the Wilderness World.

The site was founded on the basis of volunteer movement to protect and care for animals.

How it works

The main goal is to help animals, as well as the nature reserves and zoos where they are kept. we are currently working on video projects targeting pandas in China, eagles on in island near Los Angeles, alligators in Florida and gorillas in the Congo. These have total of more than 1500 mammals and reptiles.
